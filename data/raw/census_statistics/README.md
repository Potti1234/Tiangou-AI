# Census And Statistics Raw Data

Place Hong Kong Census and Statistics Department energy tables here.

These files are mainly used for territory-level annual or monthly validation of simulated demand and supply.

## Files

- `monthly_electricity_gas_by_user_type_915_91201.csv`: Table 915-91201, monthly statistics on consumption of electricity and gas by type of users.

Public table page:

https://www.censtatd.gov.hk/en/web_table.html?id=915-91201
